# Project Description & User Flow: Madhira ## 1. Project Overview **Madhira** is a specialized late-night delivery platform designed for the Nepali market. It bridges the gap between high-end curated spirits and local festive cravings. The project centers on the **"Modern Mela"** concept—bringing the vibrant energy, warmth, and celebration of a traditional festival (Mela) directly to a customer's doorstep, regardless of the hour. ### Core Value Propositions: * **Curated Spirits:** A premium selection of local and international liquors. * **The Festive Kitchen:** Freshly prepared late-night meals (momos, wings, etc.) that complement the drinks. * **Pantry Staples:** Quick-access snacks and essentials (chips, mixers) to keep the party going. * **Cultural Identity:** A design language that feels home-grown, festive, and uniquely Nepali. --- ## 2. User Personas 1. **The Late-Night Host:** Someone hosting a gathering who has run out of supplies or wants to surprise guests with high-quality snacks and drinks. 2. **The Solo Reveler:** An individual looking for a "guilty pleasure" meal or a specific bottle of wine to unwind after a long day. 3. **The Admin/Merchant:** The business owner managing inventory, tracking revenue, and ensuring timely delivery across the valley. --- ## 3. The Customer Flow (The "Mela" Journey) ### Phase 1: Gateway & Discovery 1. **Entry (Age Verification):** Due to the nature of the products, users first encounter a playful but mandatory age gate ({{DATA:SCREEN:SCREEN_12}}). 2. **Home Page Landing:** Users land on a vibrant, marigold-toned home page ({{DATA:SCREEN:SCREEN_15}}) that highlights "Late Night Revelry" and offers quick paths to Liquor, Kitchen, or Snacks. 3. **Browsing Collections:** * **Liquor Garden:** A specialized view for spirits with tasting notes and "mood" filters ({{DATA:SCREEN:SCREEN_10}}). * **Kitchen & Snacks:** A combined view for sizzling street food and gourmet chips ({{DATA:SCREEN:SCREEN_17}}). ### Phase 2: Selection & Customization 1. **Product Detail:** Users view specific items (e.g., "Yeti's Reserve") with descriptions and pricing. 2. **Cart Management:** Users add items to a persistent cart, often prompted by "Festive Bundle Deals" to increase average order value. ### Phase 3: Checkout & Fulfillment (Upcoming) 1. **Secure Checkout:** User provides delivery address (Kathmandu valley focus) and selects payment method. 2. **Live Tracking:** User receives updates as the "Mela" delivery rider approaches. --- ## 4. The Admin Flow (The "Festive Control Center") ### Phase 1: Monitoring & Strategy 1. **Performance Dashboard:** The admin views "Festive Performance" metrics, revenue trends, and critical stock alerts ({{DATA:SCREEN:SCREEN_16}}). 2. **Customer Insights:** Monitoring user loyalty, identifying top spenders (VIP Spotlights), and viewing regional growth heatmaps ({{DATA:SCREEN:SCREEN_9}}). ### Phase 2: Operations 1. **Order Management:** Processing incoming orders, assigning riders, and updating statuses from "In Prep" to "Out for Delivery" ({{DATA:SCREEN:SCREEN_7}}). 2. **Inventory Control:** Managing stock levels for 100+ items, initiating "Restocks," and creating new "Trending Combos" ({{DATA:SCREEN:SCREEN_14}}). --- ## 5. Visual Framework The project utilizes the **Madhira Utsav (Modern Mela)** design system: * **Colors:** Marigold Gold (#FFB300), Festive Red (#B5161E), and Creamy Parchment. * **Typography:** Bold, characterful Epilogue for headlines; clean Plus Jakarta Sans for UI. * **Shapes:** Extremely rounded corners (3rem) and playful, elevated card styles to mimic a friendly, celebratory atmosphere.
